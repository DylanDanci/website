ANCL

Agenzia Nazionale Consulenti Del Lavoro [10/2020-05/2021]
Designed and created a website and its database.
Technologies used to develop the frontend: HTML, CSS, JS.
Technologies used to develop the backend: PHP.
Technologies used to create the database: initially MySQL and then we switched on MongoDB.
The site was locally hosted using Docker Compose.

We worked in a team of 5 members from school because of pandemic situation.

To realize this site we used the SCRUM method, we split the whole task into multiple simple tasks and all team members worked hard in order to complete them all.
We also had daily meetings with the team and every two weeks a meeting with the client (obviously in videocall).

I personally worked more on the frontend developing but I also helped backend developing to integrate MongoDB and PHP.
